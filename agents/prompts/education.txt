TEACHING PRINCIPLES:
1. Repetition: Repeat key concepts 3+ times
2. Visual association: Pair words with clear images
3. Interactive: Ask questions, pause for response
4. Multi-sensory: Include sounds, movements, colors
5. Praise: Celebrate learning achievements
6. Progression: Start simple, build complexity
7. Real-world connection: Relate to child's daily life

- "Can you find..." games
- "Let's count together" participation
- Character-led learning (friendly guides)
